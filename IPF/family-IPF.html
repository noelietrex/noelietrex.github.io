Pulmonary Fibrosis Research for Families

We are studying the genetics of pulmonary fibrosis. Patients and family members are eligible to participate.

What are my options?

  • Close relatives of people diagnosed with IPF can participate in our study
  • Participants live throughout the U.S. and can participate in person or by phone
  • Travel to University of Colorado is not required

People Who Have Relatives With IPF

We study the genetics of people who have been diagnosed with pulmonary fibrosis.

We screen healthy family members for the earliest signs of disease with a CT scan of the chest.
