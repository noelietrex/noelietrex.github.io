About Us

We are investigating the genetics of pulmonary fibrosis and discovering ways to intervene at the earliest stages of disease before scarring becomes irreversible.

  • 1990
    Dr. Schwartz gets his first research grant to study lung fibrosis
  • 1991
    Dr. Schwartz publishes his first paper on Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis. The paper evaluates the influence of cigarette smoking on lung function in patients with IPF View More
  • 1994
    Dr. Schwartz publishes a paper discussing what determines progression of disease in IPF patients View More
  • 1996
    Dr. Schwartz publishes paper discussing lung disease related to rheumatoid arthritis View More
  • 2000
    Dr. Schwartz is awarded a grant to study the genetics of pulmonary fibrosis in families
  • 2002
    Dr. Schwartz publishes first paper on familial pulmonary fibrosis View More
  • 2011
    Dr. Schwartz and his mentee publish the first paper identifying the Muc5B variant as a key factor in pulmonary fibrosis View More
  • 2013
    Dr. Schwartz and Dr. Fingerlin publish a paper identifying multiple genes involved with IPF View More
  • 2015
    Dr. Schwartz and Dr. Yang publish a study on the epigenetics of pulmonary fibrosis View More
